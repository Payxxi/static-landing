Segment is the future of personal finance, providing businesses with a way to easily accept and hold on to USD based payments, while the customer is charged in their local currency.

People can instantly pay with a card or crypto, buy USDC, and save their money in an interest bearing account that puts them in the driving seat of their money.

*USDC is a U.S Dollar backed cryptocurrency.

Transparent and fair savings

We make use of protocols like Compound to programatically save money

Control when and how you save with our Compound integration. Start saving by funding your account with *USD via your bank card or USDC transfer. Compound is an algorithmic, autonomous interest rate protocol built for developers, to unlock a universe of open financial applications.

Compound allows you to earn interest by lending your USD balance with their protocol to someone else who provides collateral. The protocol currently holds USD $1,612,755,000.00 worth of assets with USD $896,706,681.00 borrowed.
